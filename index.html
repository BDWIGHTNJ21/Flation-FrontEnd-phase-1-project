Cadillac has been one of America's premier luxury carmakers for more than a century. It has set many standards for the industry and created many firsts in the luxury car world. It is no exaggeration to say that Cadillac's history represents the history of luxury cars in America. Lincoln is the luxury vehicle division of the American car company Ford. Marketed among the top luxury brands in the United States, Lincoln has competed closely against Cadillac since its inception.
